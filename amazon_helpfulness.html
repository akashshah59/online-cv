Amazon Helpfulness Detector

Project Information
Amazon is a leading e-commerce website and uses Machine Learning applications extensively in their products. For example, they recommend products based on your browsing history and previous purchases so as to maximize their profit (by selling you another product!). In this project we are given Amazon data. Given a review, the item for which the review is and the user who has written the review, we predict how helpful the review will be.
